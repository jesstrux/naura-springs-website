Welcome to Naura Springs



Located on East Africa Community in the Heart of Arusha city, Naura Springs Hotel is quite close to the Arusha International Conference Center (AICC) and easily accessible(45 mins) from Kilimanjaro International Airport. It is also overlooking the magnificent Mt. Meru and Mt. Kilimanjaro.


Location

The Naura Springs Hotel is located on East Africa CommunityNaura Spring Hotel Road just off the Nairobi road in the Heart of Arusha city.

Accessible

The Hotel is easily accessible, forty five minutes from Kilimanjaro International Airport (JRO), one and half hours from the Namanga Boarder and 5mins from the city center.
